Manage Your Entire Agricultural Cycle Efficiently

From sowing to distribution, our app helps you manage every stage of the agricultural process simply and effectively.

Features for Agricultural Producers

  • Sowing

    Record the amount of seeds sown, participants in the process, and the total cost.

  • Irrigation

    Schedule and monitor irrigation dates and times, with tracking of the responsible person.

  • Fumigation

    Keep track of dates, products used, and the individuals responsible for the process.

  • Harvesting

    Manage the harvested production, with tracking of the responsible personnel.

  • Distribution

    Plan delivery points, manage the quantity distributed, and control financial information.

Features for Distributors

  • Stock Management

    Monitor the available inventory in real-time for each type of agricultural product.

  • Delivery Scheduling

    Organize and schedule deliveries to various distribution points.

  • Movement Tracking

    Keep detailed control of product entries and exits, optimizing your logistics.

  • Financial Analysis

    Generate reports that will help you make informed decisions about your inventory and sales.
